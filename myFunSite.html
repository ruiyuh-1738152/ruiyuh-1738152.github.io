Spider man

A brief introduction of Spider man

This is Spider man and he is not an official avenger yet.

Spider man is a superhero being portrayed in both Marvel comics and films. The most popular version is the one played by Tom Holland. Spider man’s real name is Peter Parker, who is an orphan and is being raised by his aunt and uncle. Peter Parker becomes Spider man because he is being bitten by a poisonous spider and then he has the ability to shoot spider web and fly among between tall buildings. Spider man has joined the avengers’ team in the movie Marvel Studio produced in 2010.
